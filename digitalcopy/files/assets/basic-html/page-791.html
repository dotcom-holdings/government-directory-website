Komatiland Forests (KLF) welcomes you to our exciting world of

eco-tourism. Whether hiking, or mountain biking is your scene

we can provide the ultimate experience to make your time in

nature one of unparalleled enjoyment. Komatiland Forests was

registered as a full subsidiary of SAFCOL in mid 2002 and that

was when all the SAFCOL hiking trails in the 'old Transvaal' were

transferred to Komatiland's Eco-Tourism division. Commercial

forestry is KLF's core business currently we control an area of

more than 210 000ha of which 129 000ha has been afforested

but KLF is also committed to the concept of eco-tourism. KLF’s

eco-tourism division has a huge impact on the development

and management of:

• Overnight Hiking Trails

• Day Hiking Trails

• Lakenvlei Forest Lodge and Conference Centre

• Entavhine Kliphuis Guesthouse

• Open-Air Recreation and Picnic Spots

• Sabie Forest Industry Museum and Information Centre

• Mountain Biking Trails

• Horse Trails

You are invited to explore our site while you plan your next

visit to an eco-tourist destination.
