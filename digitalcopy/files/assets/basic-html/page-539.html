Palabora Foundation

www.pafound.za.org

The Palabora Foundation is a “not-for-profit” organisation (NPO) established in 1986 to assist communities

within Phalaborwa and outside the areas. The organisation works with private and public sector to

enable community development programmes and projects within Ba-Phalaborwa communities.

It manages a community health centre through committed qualified medical staff which focuses on

wellness, disease and outreach programmes to teaching the communities on the HIV/AIDS pandemic.

VISION

To be a leading and sustainable socio-economic development foundation for the benefit of Ba-Phalaborwa.

MISSION

Empowering the Community of Ba-Phalaborwa through development

OBJECTIVES AND GOALS

• Unlocking and sustain winning partnerships

• Enhance financial stability of Palabora Foundation

• Deliver vibrant, visible and sustainable programmes

• Nurture, grow and protect the Palabora foundation brand

• Create passionate, performance driven, winning teams
